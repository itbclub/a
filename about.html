About ITB Club

Our Story

ITB Club was born from a shared passion for the digital world. A small group of students, fascinated by the potential of AI, marketing, and creative tech, came together to create a space for hands-on learning. We wanted to go beyond textbooks and build real projects, test bold ideas, and grow as a collaborative team. Today, we are a thriving community dedicated to innovation and empowering the next generation of digital leaders.
